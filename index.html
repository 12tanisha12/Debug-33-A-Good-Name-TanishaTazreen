Welcome to the Wonderful World of Animals!

Elephant

Elephant

The elephant is the largest land animal on Earth.

It is known for its long trunk and large ears.

Hippo

Hippo

The hippopotamus is a large, mostly herbivorous mammal.

It spends most of its time in the water to keep cool.

Flamingo

Flamingo

Flamingos are known for their vibrant pink feathers and long, slender legs.

They live in large flocks and are often found in lakes.

Panda

Panda

Pandas are beloved for their distinctive black and white fur.

They primarily feed on bamboo and are native to China.

Penguin

Penguin

Penguins are flightless birds that inhabit the southern hemisphere.

They are excellent swimmers and often live in large colonies.
